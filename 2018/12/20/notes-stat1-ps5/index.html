Q1

Show that \(\hat{β}_{IV} =(X′ZW_N Z′X)^{−1}X′ZW_N Z′y\) minimizes
\[Q_N(β) = \Big[ \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N (y_i - x_i' β)z_i\Big]' W_N ​ \Big[ \frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^N (y_i - x_i' β)z_i\Big]\] ​ \[= \Big[ \frac{1}{N}Z' (y - X β)\Big]' W_N ​ \Big[ \frac{1}{N}Z' (y - X β)\Big]\]

\(Q_N(β) = \frac{1}{N^2}\Big[ (y' - β'X')Z W_N (Z'y - Z'X β)\Big]\)
——–\(= \frac{1}{N^2}\Big[ y'ZW_NZ'y - y'ZW_N Z'Xβ - β'X'Z W_N Z'y + β'X' Z W_N Z'yβ \Big]\)
——–\(= \frac{1}{N^2}\Big[ y'ZW_NZ'y - 2\big( β'X'Z W_N Z'y \big) + β'X' Z W_N Z'yβ \Big]\)
FOC
\(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial β} = -2 X'ZW_N Z'y + 2X'ZW_N Z'Xβ = 0\)
\(- X'ZW_N Z'y + X'ZW_N Z'Xβ = 0\)
\(\hat{β} = (X'ZW_N Z'X)^{-1} (X'ZW_N Z'y)\)

Q2

Derive the covariance matrix of the IV estimator \(\hat{β}_{IV} = (Z′X)^{−1}Z′y\) in the model \(y = Xβ+ε\) with homoskedastic and serially uncorrelated errors.
Argue why the correlation between instruments and regressors matter for the precision of the IV estimator.
What happens in the limiting case when this correlation approaches zero?

let \(W_N = I_N\)
\[\begin{aligned}\hat{β}_{IV} &= (X'ZZ'X)^{-1}X'ZZ'y \\ &= (Z'X)^{-1}(X'Z)^{-1}(X'Z)^{-1}Z'y \\ &= (Z'X)^{-1}Z'y \end{aligned}\]

Derive \(V(\hat{β}_{IV})\)
\[\begin{aligned}\hat{β}_{IV} &= (Z'X)^{-1}Z'(Xβ+ε) \\ &= (Z'X)^{-1}Z'Xβ + (Z'X)^{-1}Z'ε$, $V(ε) = σ^2 I \end{aligned}\]
\(\implies\)
\[\begin{aligned}V(\hat{β}_{IV}) &= V(β + (Z'X)^{-1}Z'ε) \\ &= (Z'X)^{-1}Z'σ^2 I [(Z'X)^{-1}Z']' \\ &= σ^2 (Z'X)^{-1}Z'Z(X'Z)^{-1}\end{aligned}\]

Q3

Derive the GIVE.
Start with the first stage regression \(X = Zγ + v\) and obtain its fitted values \(\hat{X}\).
Show that the first stage fit \(\hat{X}\) is given by \(P_ZX\) with \(P_Z = Z(Z′Z)^{−1}Z′\).
Then consider the OLS estimator in the second stage (\(y = Xβ+ε\)) based on fitted values \(\hat{X}\) instead of \(X\) from the first stage regression, i.e. \(y = \hat{X} β + ε\).
Show that the resulting estimator equals the GIVE.
Derive the covariance matrix of the GIVE under homoskedasticity and absence of autocorrelation.
Compare your result to Problem 2.

\(\hat{γ} = (Z'Z)^{-1}ZX\)
fitted values:
\[\begin{aligned}\hat{X} &= Z \hat{γ} \\ &= Z(Z'Z)^{-1}ZX = P_Z X\end{aligned}\]

check \(P_Z\) is symmetric:
\(P_Z' = [Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z']' = Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z' = P_Z\)

check \(P_Z\) is idopotent:
\(P_ZP_Z = Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z' Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z' = P_Z\)

second stage estimator:
\(y = \hat{X}β+ ε\)
\(b = (\hat{X}'\hat{X})^{-1} \hat{X}'y\)
so \(\hat{X} = P_Z X\) and \(\hat{X}' = X'P_Z' = X'P_Z\)
\[\begin{aligned}b &= (X'P_Z P_ZX)^{-1} X'P_Z y \\ &= (X'P_ZX)^{-1} X'P_Z y \\ &= \big( X'Z(Z'Z)^{-1}ZX\big)^{-1} X'Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z y \end{aligned}\]

Variance:
\[\begin{aligned}V(b) &= σ^2 (\hat{X}' \hat{X})^{-1} \\ &= σ^2 (X'P_Z X)^{-1} \\ &= σ^2 (X'Z(Z'Z)^{-1}Z X)^{-1} \\ &= σ^2 (Z'X)^{-1}Z'Z(X'Z)^{-1}\end{aligned}\]

Q4

Consider the model \(y^∗_i = x'_i β + ε_i\)
where \(ε_i ∼^{iid} N(0,σ^2_ε)\) and is independent of \(x_i\).
In addition, \(x_i ∼^{iid} N(μ,σ^2_x)\).
The dependent variable is observed with measurement error, i.e., \(y_i = y_i^∗ + v_i\)
where \(v_i ∼^{iid} N(0,σ^2_v)\) is independent of both \(x_j\) and \(ε_j ∀j\).
(The unobserved yi is sometimes called a latent variable - an unobserved, but otherwise important component.)
Regress now \(y_i\) on \(x_i\) to obtain an OLS estimate \(\tilde{β}\)

a)

Is \(\tilde{β}\) a consistent estimator of \(β\) ?

rewrite \(y_i - v_i = x_i' β ε_i\)
\(\implies\)
\(y_i = x_i' β ε_i + v_i\) where \(ε_i\) and \(v_i\) are uncorrelated.
let \(ε_i + v_i = u_i\), \(u_i ∼^{iid}N(0, σ^2_ε + σ^2_v)\)

OLS estimator:
\(\tilde{β} = (\sum^N_{i=1} x_i x_i')^{-1}\sum^N_{i=1} x_iy_i\)
– $ = β + (^N_{i=1} x_i x_i’){-1}N_{i=1} x_iu_i$

since \(ε_i\) and \(v_i\) are uncorrelated, \(\text{plim}\sum^N_{i=1} x_iu_i = 0\) (\(x'x\) converges to a stable and well-defined likelihood matrix Σ)
as long as \(\text{plim}\sum^N_{i=1} x_ix_i' > 0\), \(\tilde{β}\) is consistent

b)

(How) Does your answer change if \(ε_i\) and \(v_i\) are correlated?

only the variance of \(u_i\) changes as follows:
\(var(u_i) = σ^2_ε + ε^2_v + 2cov(ε_i, v_i)\)
but importantly, consistency remains
